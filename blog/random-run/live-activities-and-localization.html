Live Activities, Dynamic Island & Now in French and German

Random Run exists for a simple reason: running the same route every day is boring. You tell us how far or how long you want to run, and we generate three random loop routes starting and ending right where you are. One tap, a new part of your city to explore. That's it.

Today's update makes that experience even better.

Run with your screen locked

Random Run now supports Live Activities and Dynamic Island. That means your current turn-by-turn direction, distance remaining, and progress all show up right on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island — no need to unlock your phone or leave the app open.

Switch to your music app, lock your screen, put your phone in your pocket. Glance down when you feel a tap and your next turn is right there. Running with Random Run has never felt this seamless.

Merci! Danke!

We've been blown away by the interest from runners in France and Germany. You asked, and we listened — Random Run is now fully localized in French and German. Every screen, every label, every notification. If your device is set to French or German, Random Run will feel right at home.

This is just the beginning. More languages are coming, and if yours isn't supported yet, let us know — your feedback directly shapes what we work on next.

Still free. Still simple.

Random Run is completely free to download and use. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads. Just open the app, pick your distance or duration, and go find a new favourite street.
