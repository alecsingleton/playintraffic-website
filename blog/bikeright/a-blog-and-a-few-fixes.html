A New Blog and a Few Fixes

Most of our time goes into building features for BikeRight, but every so often we turn our attention to the website. This week we shipped a handful of improvements worth mentioning.

A place to follow along

You're reading it. The BikeRight landing page now has a "What's New" section with blog cards linking to full posts. Going forward, this is where we'll share feature updates, release notes, and the occasional behind-the-scenes look at how we build the app.

No newsletter signup required — just check back whenever you're curious.

Housekeeping

A couple of small but important fixes landed alongside the blog. The App Store download link now points to the correct full listing URL instead of a generic placeholder, and BikeRight pages use their own favicon rather than accidentally borrowing Random Run's icon. Small things, but they matter.

What's next

We're back to working on the app itself. If you have ideas for features, routes, or anything else, reach out at playintraffic.ca@gmail.com. We read every message.
