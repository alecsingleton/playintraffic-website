Random Run Now Works on Apple Watch

Random Run is built on one idea: running should surprise you. Tell us how far or how long you want to go, and we'll generate three random loop routes right from where you're standing. No planning, no overthinking — just pick one and discover a part of your city you've never seen before.

With v1.3, you don't even need your phone to do it.

Your wrist is all you need

Random Run now runs natively on Apple Watch. Generate routes, choose your favourite, and get turn-by-turn directions — all from your wrist. Your iPhone can stay at home, in your locker, wherever. The Watch handles the entire experience on its own.

That means lighter runs. No armband, no bouncing phone in your pocket, no fumbling with a screen while you're moving. Just a quick glance at your wrist when a turn is coming up, and you're on your way.

Everything you'd expect

This isn't a stripped-down companion app. You get the full Random Run experience on your wrist: pick distance or duration, generate three routes, preview them, start navigating. Your pace, distance, and elapsed time are all tracked and saved — the same data you'd see on your phone, synced automatically.

Still free. Always.

Random Run is completely free — on iPhone and now on Apple Watch. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads. Just lace up, tap your watch, and go find your next favourite route.
